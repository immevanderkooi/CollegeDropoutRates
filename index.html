Why Student Drop Out of College

The problem we are trying to solve is why students drop out of college, to better understand what administrators can do to keep students enrolled. This is important because about 33% dropped out of college in 2023, which is an increase from previous years. Higher education and education equality are so important for the progress of society as a whole, so we aim to better understand what is leading to dropouts to employ strategies in universities that can prevent these dropouts in the future.

Plan

We plan on running a logistic regression to find out which variables and factors such as demographic, socioeconomic, and academic performance most contribute to graduation versus dropout. We also plan to use either a decision tree or a random forest to further visualize and understand our findings.
